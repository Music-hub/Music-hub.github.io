Share the music sheet

MUSICHUB

Description

Musichub is a platform for sharing music sheet.

You can view other's collection online.

Moreover, you can co-edit the sheet to make it better!

Feature

Sharing music sheet
You can view other's music sheet online and you can fork the collection to your account after his or her permission.
Edit online
After forking other's collection, you can edit the sheet online. The owner of the origin sheet will be noticed about the changes you make.
Audition online
You can audition the music according to your sheet online to make sure if the change you make is desirable, and download the sheet to your PC.
